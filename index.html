Sleeping

Sleeping is an essential need for both the body and mind to stay healthy and energized. Having enough rest helps to restore energy after a long day of activities, improves focus, and maintains emotional balance. Sleep also strengthens the immune system and gives the brain time to process and store memories more effectively.

Playing Games

Playing games is a fun way to relax and release stress. This activity not only provides entertainment but also sharpens critical thinking skills, quick decision-making, and hand-eye coordination. Additionally, playing games with friends encourages teamwork and strengthens social connections while working together to achieve goals in the game.

Evening Walks aroung GMI

Taking an evening walk around the GMI area is a relaxing activity that benefits both physical and mental health. The calm and refreshing atmosphere of the campus surroundings makes the walk more enjoyable, while the movement helps improve heart health and muscle strength. It is also a good opportunity to unwind after classes, spend time with friends, and appreciate the peaceful environment of GMI as the day transitions into night.

Projects

Project Bugatti

The Bugatti Chiron is a limited-production, W16-powered hypercar known for its extreme performance, including a 0-100 km/h sprint in about 2.4-2.5 seconds and an electronically limited top speed of 420 km/h or 440 km/h for the Super Sport model. It features advanced components, such as a quad-turbocharged 8.0-liter W16 engine and an all-wheel-drive system, with different variants like the Chiron Sport and Pur Sport offering specialized handling and aerodynamics.

Project LaFerrari

The Ferrari LaFerrari is a hybrid hypercar, Ferrari's first production car to use a hybrid system called HY-KERS. Launched in 2013, it features a powerful V12 engine combined with an electric motor for a total output of 963 horsepower. The LaFerrari was a trailblazer, integrating F1-inspired technology and aerodynamics into a road car, and was followed by the open-top LaFerrari Aperta model.

Project BMW

The BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance sports coupé featuring a powerful BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder petrol engine, offering exceptional driving dynamics derived from motorsport. It includes features like M specific instrument clusters, Head-Up Displays, and an optional M xDrive all-wheel-drive system with an Active M Differential. The design is athletic, with optional M specific Carbon features for both the exterior and interior, and it is available with M Performance Parts for further personalization.

Project Lamborghini

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ is an ultra-high-performance, track-focused variant of the Aventador supercar, featuring a 6.5L naturally aspirated V12 engine, advanced Aerodinamica Lamborghini Attiva (ALA) (Active Lamborghini Aerodynamics) system, and extensive use of lightweight carbon fiber. It holds the production car lap record at the Nürburgring-Nordschleife and combines a powerful, high-revving engine with futuristic aerodynamics and a driver-centric, luxurious interior design.

Project Audi

The Audi R8 is a mid-engine supercar known for its performance, racing heritage, and driver-focused design, which shares parts with the R8 GT3 LMS race car. Launched in the U.S. in 2007, it was inspired by the Le Mans quattro concept car and featured a mid-mounted V10 engine, quattro all-wheel drive, and a lightweight aluminum space frame.
